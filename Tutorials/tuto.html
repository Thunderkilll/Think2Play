Dots and Boxes Rules.

Object of the board game.

Take more boxes than your opponent. You move by connecting two dots with a line. When you place the last ‘wall’ of a single square (box), the box is yours and the corresponding image appears inside the box. The players move in turn, but whenever a player takes a box (s)he must move again. The board game ends when all 36 boxes have been taken. The player with the most boxes wins.

You click to select

To win the square you need to be the one to close it with your color

If you close a square you get extra turn

REMEMBER THE CARDS Rules.

memory game.

12 pictures (6 pairs) will be randomly shown in the screen faced-up for 5 seconds then faced-down, and the child will have to get the right pairs. As in the first game , the score would be saved in the database and shared with other users (the score for this game is the time that the child took to get the pairs right).

You Select the level of difficulty

The game will give you 5 seconds to memorise the cards

You need to match the cards
