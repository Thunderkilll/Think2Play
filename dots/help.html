Dots and Boxes Rules.

Object of the board game.

Take more boxes than your opponent. You move by connecting two dots with a line. When you place the last ‘wall’ of a single square (box), the box is yours and the corresponding image appears inside the box. The players move in turn, but whenever a player takes a box (s)he must move again. The board game ends when all 36 boxes have been taken. The player with the most boxes wins.
